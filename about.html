About

"Beauty is confidence in bloom."

Pro Nails is a family-owned beauty salon and spa based in Waynesboro, VA. At Pro Nails, we are more than just a salon and spa – we are a sanctuary for self-care and confidence. We specializing in nail care (manicures and pedicures), while also offering luxurious massages and waxing services designed to elevate your overall well-being.

Our vision extends beyond providing exceptional services – we strive to build a lifelong relationship of trust and care with our clients. At Pro Nails, it’s not just about beauty; it’s about creating a community where you feel valued, pampered, and your absolute best every time you visit.

How did we get here?

Pro Nails began its journey in the early 2000s in Staunton, Virginia. The salon was born from a shared passion for beauty and creating a sanctuary where clients could feel their most confident. With unwavering years of hard work, the founders poured their hearts into building Pro Nails from the ground up.

Through years of hard work and a deep connection to their community, they were able to open a new location in Waynesboro, Virginia. For nearly a decade, this vibrant space has been the heart of Pro Nails, welcoming clients to indulge in luxurious treatments and unparalleled care.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure every customer feels satisfied, confident, and their most beautiful. We understand that by entrusting us with your time and beauty – whether it’s for nail care, body hair, or relaxation – you are placing your confidence in us. That’s why we prioritize creating an unparalleled experience for each and every client.

Every detail of our service is carefully crafted to enhance your sense of comfort and spa indulgence. We use essential oils, scented flower petals, and nourishing lotions/scrubs to enhance customer experiences and provide a soothing and rejuvenating atmosphere that leaves you feeling refreshed, pampered, and truly cared for.

"Where quality meets your every need."

At Pro Nails, we offer a comprehensive range of services designed to meet your beauty and relaxation needs:

Manicure Services

We specialize in various manicure options, including acrylic, dip powder, and gel applications, accommodating any nail shape, length, or repair needs, including older nail set removals or adjustments. Here at Pro Nails, we offer a wide range of manicure options, all of which include trim, shape, buff, and polish. Many services also incorporate cuticle maintenance, lotion massages, sugar scrubs, and paraffin wax treatments for a complete hand-care experience.

Pedicure Services

We offer a wide range of pedicure options, each including trim, shape, cuticle care, and polish. Enhancements like warm towel wraps, sugar scrubs, detoxifying masks, and candle oil massages elevate your experience for ultimate relaxation and care.

Waxing Services

We provide a variety of waxing services, ranging from eyebrows to full Brazilian waxing, ensuring smooth and flawless results tailored to your needs.

Massage Services

Relax and rejuvenate with our soothing foot massages, designed to relieve tension and enhance your overall well-being. We also offer specialized services for men and children, ensuring everyone feels welcome and pampered at Pro Nails. Whether you’re seeking luxurious treatments or quick touch-ups, our services are designed to leave you feeling refreshed, confident, and beautiful.
